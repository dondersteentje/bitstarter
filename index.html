Delivr.us let's people help each other out.



Everyday life is full of surprises.

Your son forgets his football socks (again...) and the game is on in 15 minutes. Your wife needs her phone, but she left it on the charger at home. Your friend urgently needs her cocktail dress back, something with 'a really -- REALLY-- hot date tonight', but you're in the middle of your neighbour's toddlers' party and the room is loaded with drooling babies in need of instant attention.

You know, it happens all the time. Nothing serious, but...

What if you could just pick up your smartphone and send for help right away?

Ask somebody to get your son's socks to the football field? Or take your friend's dress and deliver it at her job location during the day?

That's what we enable with Delivr.us. We're building a network of people that help each other out, with everyday small and nearby deliveries in their hometown. Delivr.us is build on a local city per city basis.

We match nearby Delivrers in realtime.


The 'Delivrers' are the people living in your town. Cycling to their schools, jobs, getting some grocery or visiting a friend. That may well be a route that covers your A to B need. Our service matches the nearby delivrers to you in realtime.

You decide what you want to give for the job.

That could range from a homebaked lemon pie up to a small payment.

By taking up your package at A and delivering it at its destination B, Delivrers make a small buck, get spoiled with delicious pie, meet interesting people (even if-at the moment- a little stressed out) or just help someone out. We are a network of movers and shakers.

Build Good Karma

Here is a little secret: It's really great to do. Helping someone out gives your own day a huge positive boost. Besides good Delivrers get positive ratings and level up in the system. That's how you can gain a visible trustworthy profile.. So how about it? Delivrer may just as well be you on the next day!
